Question: How do I adjust the carburetor on a Model T?

To adjust the carburetor on a Model T, follow these steps:

1. Insert the end of the rod through the throttle lever "B".
2. Lock the rod in place by inserting a cotter pin through its end.
3. Install the carburetor adjusting rod by inserting its head through a slot in the dash.
4. Place the forked end of the rod "C" through the head of the carburetor needle valve.
5. Lock the rod in position by inserting a cotter key through its end.
