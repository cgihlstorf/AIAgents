Question: What is the purpose of the Main Street Parity Act?

The purpose of the Main Street Parity Act is to provide a federal tax credit for businesses that invest in infrastructure projects, such as roads and bridges. This act was passed by Congress on December 18, 2017, and signed into law by President Donald Trump on January 3, 2018.
