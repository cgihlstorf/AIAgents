Question: What is the purpose of the Main Street Parity Act?

The purpose of the Main Street Parity Act is to modify the criteria for loans for plant acquisition, construction, conversion or expansion by establishing an equity requirement at 10 percent, making these loans more aligned with the 504 standardization programs. This act aims to provide small businesses across the country with a new opportunity to step forward and create economic opportunities within their communities. Additionally, it is described as "common sense" and a "clear fix" that addresses the need for better alignment between loan requirements and modern economic realities.
