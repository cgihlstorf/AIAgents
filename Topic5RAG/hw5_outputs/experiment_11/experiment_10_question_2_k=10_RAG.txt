For a complete tune-up, you would need:

1. Tools such as wrenches, pliers, and screwdrivers to tighten bolts and screws.
2. A multimeter to measure voltage levels and continuity.
3. A socket set to turn various types of fasteners.
4. An adjustable wrench for larger bolts.
5. A torque wrench for precise tightening of certain components.
6. Wire strippers to prepare wires for connections.
7. Electrical connectors and wire cutters if needed for wiring adjustments.
8. A bench vise or other tool to hold components during installation.
9. A level to ensure proper alignment of parts.
10. A set of gauges like dial indicators for precision measurements. 

These tools will help in performing detailed checks and adjustments according to the specific instructions given in the context.
