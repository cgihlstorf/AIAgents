AI systems can provide key competitive advantages to undertakings by improving prediction, optimising operations, and personalising digital solutions. They also support socially and environmentally beneficial outcomes across various sectors including healthcare, agriculture, food safety, education and training, media, sports, culture, infrastructure management, energy, transport and logistics, public services, security, justice, and resource and energy efficiency. Additionally, AI literacy helps ensure informed decision-making about AI systems, particularly concerning their impact on fundamental rights, health, and safety. These benefits contribute to enhancing societal participation and quality of life.
