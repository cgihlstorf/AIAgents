People have the right to privacy and protection of personal data throughout the lifecycle of the AI system. The principles of data minimization and data protection by design and by default apply when personal data are processed according to Union data protection law. Additionally, relevant competent authorities providing or supporting access to data can help ensure high-quality data for training, validation, and testing of AI systems. Furthermore, the use of AI systems should not undermine fundamental rights to data protection and privacy, especially in areas like essential private and public services and benefits necessary for participation in society or improving one's standard of living. Lastly, the fundamental right to the protection of personal data is safeguarded by specific regulations and directives.
