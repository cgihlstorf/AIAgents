The main goals of the EU AI Act include improving the functioning of the internal market, promoting the uptake of human-centric and trustworthy AI, ensuring a high level of protection of health, safety, fundamental rights enshrined in the Charter, including democracy, the rule of law, and environmental protection against harmful effects of AI systems in the Union, and preventing restrictions on the development, marketing, and use of AI systems imposed by Member States without explicit authorization under this regulation. Additionally, it aims to ensure the free movement, cross-border, of AI-based goods and services.
