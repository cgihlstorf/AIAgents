Question: How do I adjust the carburetor on a Model T?

The carburetor is adjusted by turning the adjustment screw located at the top of the carburetor. This screw controls the amount of fuel that enters the engine, and can be turned either clockwise or counterclockwise to increase or decrease the flow of fuel.
