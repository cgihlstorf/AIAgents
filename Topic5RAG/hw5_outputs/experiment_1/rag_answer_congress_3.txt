Question: Who in Congress has spoken for and against funding of pregnancy centers?

Ms. Dexter of Oregon has spoken against funding of pregnancy centers. She stated, "Mr. Speaker, I rise today in strong opposition to the proposal Republicans voted on to take money from families in need and divert it to anti-abortion crisis pregnancy centers." Additionally, Mr. Schneider spoke in favor of funding crisis pregnancy centers, stating, "My Republican colleagues are saying that these centers help women." 

Congressional representatives have taken opposing stances regarding the funding of pregnancy centers. The context indicates that Ms. Dexter opposes the funding while Mr. Schneider supports it.
