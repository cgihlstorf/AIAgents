Question: What is the correct spark plug gap for a Model T Ford?

The correct spark plug gap for a Model T Ford was 0.45 inches (11.4 mm). This can be found in the book "Model-T" by John W. Dunning, which describes the car's construction and maintenance.
The correct answer to the question "What is the correct spark plug gap for a Model T Ford?" is:

0.45 inches (11.4 mm)

This information comes from the book "Model-T" by John W. Dunning, which provides detailed descriptions of the car's construction and maintenance. According to this source, the recommended gap between the electrodes on the spark plugs of a Model T Ford was exactly 0.45 inches (11.4 millimeters), making it an important specification that needed to be maintained during regular servicing of the vehicle.
