The right to privacy and to protection of personal data must be guaranteed throughout the entire lifecycle of the AI system. This includes ensuring compliance with Union data protection laws, such as GDPR and ePrivacy, and implementing measures like anonymization, encryption, and technology that allows algorithms to be brought to data without transmission or copying of raw or structured data, all within an appropriate institutional governance framework. Additionally, it emphasizes the importance of protecting against unauthorized alterations or unintended harm to the use or performance of AI systems.
